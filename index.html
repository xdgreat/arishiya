Our History

Since 2022, House of Paws has been a pillar of veterinary care in Suva, Fiji. With a longstanding commitment to the health and happiness of pets, our experienced team has provided compassionate and personalized services to the local community. As we continue to serve our beloved furry friends, we are proud of our heritage and look forward to many more years of exceptional care.
